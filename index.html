How to Play

Welcome to Word Unscramble 2! This is remake of my previous Word Unscramble game.

The rules are simple. You have to unscramble the given word. You have to set how many lives you want for a new game and you have to unscramble a total of 5 scrambled words. The maximum lives you can set is 15. If you lose all your lives, you lose. Good luck!
